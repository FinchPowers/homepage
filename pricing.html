How do we compare?

Other CI services charge you extra for concurrency. This means that if your app has 5 services and each service takes 2 minutes to deploy; your entire app will take 5 x 2 minutes = 10 minutes to deploy when using one concurrent build server. Seed on the other hand will build all your services concurrently, so the entire deployment takes 2 minutes.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are build minutes?

    Build minutes are the amount of time a build process takes to complete in minutes.

  • How are build minutes calculated on an account?

    Free accounts have a limit of 500 build minutes a month. While the paid plans give you 1500 build minutes per user on the account. So if your account has 10 users; your account has 10 x 1500 build minutes = 150 000 build minutes. If you need more build minutes on your account, add another user to the account.
